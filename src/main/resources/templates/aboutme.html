About me

With the addition of a video game console in our home, my brothers and I used to spend as many hours playing sports in front of the tv as we did outside. The good ol' fashion button mashin' of my youth was no small part of the inspiration for the 2012 Summer Games interactive doodles. (In fact, collaborator Marcin Wichary even wired up these doodles with the then newly released Gamepad API, allowing users to mash actual buttons.)
